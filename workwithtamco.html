Job Opportunities


Game Developer

are responsible for designing, coding, and implementing game features and mechanics. They work closely with artists, designers, and other developers to bring game concepts to life. Their role involves troubleshooting and optimizing code to ensure smooth gameplay and high performance.

Graphic Designer/Concept Artist

create visually compelling graphics for games, including character designs, environments, and user interfaces. They collaborate with game designers to develop concept art and ensure visual consistency across the game. Their work involves a mix of creativity and technical skills to bring artistic visions to reality.

3D Animator

are tasked with creating and animating three-dimensional characters and objects for games. They bring static models to life by adding movement and expressions, ensuring that animations are smooth and engaging. Their role requires a keen eye for detail and a strong understanding of animation principles.

Social Media Manager

are responsible for managing and growing a company's social media presence. They create and implement strategies to engage with the community, promote games, and drive traffic to the companyâ€™s website. Sound Designer

create and implement audio elements for games, including sound effects, dialogue, and background music. They work with the development team to ensure that audio enhances the overall gaming experience and fits seamlessly with the visual and narrative aspects of the game. Their role involves both technical skills and a creative ear.

Software Engineer

develop and maintain the software that powers games, including game engines and tools. They write and debug code, integrate new technologies, and ensure that software components function correctly. Their role is crucial for creating efficient and reliable game systems.

Network Engineer

design and manage the network infrastructure that supports multiplayer games and online features. They work to ensure stable and secure connections, troubleshoot network issues, and optimize performance for a smooth gaming experience. Their role involves both technical expertise and problem-solving skills.
